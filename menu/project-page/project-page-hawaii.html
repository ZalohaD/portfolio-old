Hawaii specializes in creating web designes. Described as art meets science and providing a spiritual sense of place, their designs harmonize workspace activity of all types and create a legacy that reflects and symbolizes their response to the many challenges which designers are continually faced with. I was retained to redesign the website and modernize it to better reflect Hawaii' high-end positioning.

The website has become a powerful portfolio tool that facilitates decision-making and communication by presenting notable achievements, the range of products, and services offered by the Hawaii team. Inspired by architecture and refinement, the art direction emancipates the brand while maintaining its identity.
