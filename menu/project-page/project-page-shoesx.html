ShoesX



service :

Web Development

started :

25 December 2020

completed :

17 April 2021

I developed Shoesx, a contemporary Student work-inspired offering affordable, quality shoes . “We believe that product can be smart, fun and evoke emotion. Designed to not only look good but have purpose, function well and be built to last.”

Initially retained to develop a showcase website to launch the ShoesX brand, My mandate was to build the digital brand and develop both the user experience and an intuitive platform to present the product range. The platform having already been designed with the potential for online sales, PayPal technology was incorporated in a second phase to transform the platform into a complete e-commerce experience.
